Mae's Sweet Potato Pie



  • Anna's Lemon Squares
  • Sara's Peanut Butter Cookies
  • Mae's Sweet Potato Pie
  • Mary's Killer Karrot Kake
  • Becky's Asian Chex Mix

Ingredients

  • 3 cooked sweet potatoes
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons nutmeg
  • 1 8 oz. can evaporated milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • ¼ lb (1 stick) butter, melted
  • 1 9” prepared pie crust or your favorite pie crust recipe

    • Directions


    Peel and mash sweet potatoes. Add eggs and mix. Add and mix sugar, nutmeg, evaporated milk, vanilla, and butter together until all ingredients are incorporated. Pour mixture into pie shell.


    Bake at 350° in preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
