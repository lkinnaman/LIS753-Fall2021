Anna's Lemon Squares


Ingredients


• ½ cup butter
• 1 cup + 2 tablespoons flour
• ¼ cup powdered sugar
• 1 cup granulated sugar
• 2 eggs, well beaten
• ¼ teaspoon salt
• 3 tablespoons lemon juice
• ½ teaspoon baking powder

Directions


While preparing the crust, preheat oven to 350°.

Crust Cream butter and powdered sugar until light and fluffy. Add 1 cup of flour and mix. Spread mixture into ungreased 9” pan. Bake at 350° for 10 minutes.

Filling While crust is baking, beat sugar into eggs. Add salt, lemon, baking powder, 2 tablespoons flour and mix well. Spread over baked crust.

Bake at 350° for 30 minutes. Let cool completely, then dust with powdered sugar. Cut into squares.
