Sarah's Peanut Butter Cookies


Ingredients


• 1 cup peanut butter (smooth or crunchy)
• 1 cup granulated sugar
• 1 large egg

Directions


Preheat oven to 350°

Combine the peanut butter, sugar and egg. Mix until smooth.

Drop dough by the spoonful onto the baking sheet. Use a fork to make crosshatches on top of each cookie before baking. Bake at 350° for 6 to 8 minutes. Cookies should be barely browned on the bottom. Allow to cool 5-10 minutes before removing cookies from baking sheet.
