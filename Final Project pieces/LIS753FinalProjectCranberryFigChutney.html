Becky's Cranberry Fig Chutney


Ingredients


• 2 12 oz. bags fresh cranberries, approximately 6 cups
• 2 medium oranges, unpeeled, seeded, and chopped
• 8 dried figs, chopped
• ¼ cup raisins
• ¼ cup pistachio nuts, shelled and toasted
• ½ cup onion, finely chopped
• 3 tablespoons fresh ginger, finely chopped
• 3 cups granulated sugar
• 1 teaspoon salt
• 1 teaspoon cinnamon
• 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
• 1 teaspoon dried mustard

Directions

Cook all ingredients in a large, heavy non-aluminum pot over medium-low heat. Stir until sugar dissolves. Increase heat and boil until cranberries pop, 3-5 minutes, or longer (cook until it is the texture you want, however long it takes).

*will keep up to6 months tightly covered in refrigerator.
