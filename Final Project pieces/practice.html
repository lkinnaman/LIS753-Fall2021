The recipes featured here have been selected from a collection gathered over a lifetime from friends and acquaintances. While most of these people are no longer in my life, their influence lingers through the recipes they have left behind. There is a wide range of complexity here, from Sarah's simple, yet intensely flavorful three-ingredient peanut butter cookies to Becky's savory, luscious cranberry fig chutney. Try out whatever catches your fancy with full expectation that the results will be delicious.
