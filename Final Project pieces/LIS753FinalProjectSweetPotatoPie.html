Mae's Sweet Potato Pie


Ingredients


• 3 cooked sweet potatoes
• 3 eggs
• 1 cup sugar
• 1 teaspoon nutmeg
• 1 15 oz. can evaporated milk
• 1 teaspoon vanilla
• ¼ lb (1 stick) butter, melted
• 1 9” prepared pie crust or your favorite pie crust recipe

Directions


Peel and mash sweet potatoes. Add eggs and mix together. Add and mix in sugar, nutmeg, evaporated milk, vanilla, and butter together until all ingredients are incorporated. Pour mixture into pie shell. Bake at 350° in preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes or until toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.

Extra batter can be saved by freezing.
